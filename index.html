Study title

Sonographic characterisation of uterine fibroids and its relationship with the quantitative severity of abnormal uterine bleeding among women in Ibadan, Nigeria.

Principal investigator

Olaoye — BSc, MSc (Anatomy), PGD Ultrasonography (RRBN)
Department of Radiology and Imaging Sciences, Molly Specialist Hospital, Ibadan.

Purpose of this study

You are being invited to take part in a research study that aims to understand the relationship between the ultrasound features of uterine fibroids and the severity of abnormal menstrual bleeding. Your participation will help improve the management of women with fibroids in our setting.

What your participation involves

  • Completing this questionnaire about your menstrual and medical history (approximately 10–15 minutes).
  • Undergoing a pelvic ultrasound scan as part of your routine care.
  • Completing a pictorial blood assessment chart (PBAC) during your next menstrual cycle.

Confidentiality

All information you provide is strictly confidential. Your name will not appear on any research document. You will be identified only by a participant code. Data will be stored securely and accessible only to the research team.

Voluntary participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without any effect on the care you receive at this facility.

Benefits and risks

There are no direct risks to you from participating. The findings from this study may benefit future patients with similar conditions.

Contact

If you have any questions about this study, please speak with the sonographer or attending clinician before proceeding.
